“Mallari” is a film that delves into
the life of Fr. Juan Severino Mallari,
the lone recorded serial
killer in Philippine history during the
1800s. This gripping narrative unfolds
with precision, blending accurate
historical events with psychological
terrors skillfully portrayed by
Piolo Pascual.
Piolo Pascual, J.C. Santos,
Elisse Joson, Janella Salvador
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
GomBurZa is a historical film that
will move and inspire today's
Filipinos. It tells the story of three
martyr priests - Mariano Gomez, Jose
Burgos, and Jacinto Zamora - who
were executed by Spain in 1872
falsely accused of sedition and treason.
Dante Rivero, Cedrick Juan,
Enchong Dee
